Privacy Data Request

AI Nutrition App users may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of their personal information.

How to submit a request

Send an email from the email address associated with your AI Nutrition App account.

Email: ainutritionapp.privacy@gmail.com

Information to include

  • Your account email address
  • The type of request: access, correction, export, or deletion
  • A brief description of the information involved

We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing the request. Do not send passwords, payment card details, identity documents, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

We will respond within the period required by applicable privacy law. Certain information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or transaction-record purposes.

For complete account deletion instructions, visit Delete Account.
